Swara is an intelligent AI agent that automatically engages with your social media audience daily, sparking conversations about AI, Robotics, Space, and Climate Change.

## 🎯 Features

- **Daily Automated Questions**: Posts thought-provoking questions about AI, Robotics, Space, and Climate
- **Smart Engagement**: Automatically replies to mentions and comments with context-aware responses
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Analyzes audience sentiment and engagement patterns
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram (extensible for more)
- **Content Calendar**: Automated scheduling with topic rotation
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track engagement metrics and audience insights
- **News Integration**: Fetches latest news to keep conversations relevant
- **Response Categorization**: Organizes and tags audience responses
- **Export & Reporting**: Generates daily/weekly reports in multiple formats
